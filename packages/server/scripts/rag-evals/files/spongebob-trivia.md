# SpongeBob SquarePants Trivia

## The Show

- SpongeBob SquarePants premiered on Nickelodeon on **May 1, 1999**.
- The show was created by **Stephen Hillenburg**, a marine science educator and animator.
- Hillenburg originally didn't want SpongeBob to have a romantic interest, which is why Sandy and SpongeBob are just friends.
- The show is set in the underwater city of **Bikini Bottom**, which is located beneath Bikini Atoll in the Pacific Ocean.
- The original pilot episode was called **"Help Wanted"**.
- SpongeBob was almost named **SpongeBoy**, but that name was already trademarked by a mop company.

## Characters

### SpongeBob SquarePants
- SpongeBob works as a **fry cook at the Krusty Krab**.
- His address is **124 Conch Street**.
- He has a pet snail named **Gary**, whose meow sounds like a cat.
- SpongeBob's favorite hobby is **jellyfishing**.
- He attended **Mrs. Puff's Boating School** but repeatedly failed his driving test.
- SpongeBob holds **374 consecutive boating school diplomas** in one episode — but always fails the actual road test.

### Patrick Star
- Patrick lives under a **rock** at 120 Conch Street.
- He is SpongeBob's best friend and is consistently depicted as dim-witted but lovable.
- Patrick's full name is **Patrick Star**.
- He briefly became famous in the episode **"I'm With Stupid"**.
- Patrick's parents are **Herb and Margie Star**.

### Squidward Tentacles
- Despite his name, Squidward is actually an **octopus** — he has six tentacles, not eight.
- He lives in an **Easter Island head** between SpongeBob and Patrick's homes at 122 Conch Street.
- Squidward's greatest passion is playing the **clarinet**, though he is widely regarded as terrible at it.
- He works as the **cashier** at the Krusty Krab.
- Squidward considers himself a sophisticated artist and occasionally showcases his self-portrait paintings.

### Sandy Cheeks
- Sandy is a **squirrel from Texas** who lives underwater in a glass air dome called the **Treedome**.
- She wears an **astronaut-like suit** with a helmet when outside her dome.
- Sandy is an expert in **karate, science, and inventing**.
- Her full name is **Sandra Jennifer Cheeks**.
- She works as a scientist and extreme-sports enthusiast.

### Mr. Krabs
- Mr. Krabs' first name is **Eugene H. Krabs**.
- He is the owner of the **Krusty Krab** restaurant.
- His daughter is **Pearl**, a sperm whale.
- Mr. Krabs is obsessed with **money** above almost everything else.
- His arch-rival is **Plankton**, who runs the rival restaurant the **Chum Bucket**.

### Plankton
- Plankton's full name is **Sheldon James Plankton**.
- His computer wife is named **Karen** (a sentient computer).
- His singular goal throughout the series is to steal the **Krabby Patty secret formula**.
- Plankton attended the same school as Mr. Krabs and they were once childhood friends.
- The Chum Bucket's address is **across the street from the Krusty Krab**.

### Mrs. Puff
- Mrs. Puff runs the **Mrs. Puff's Boating School**.
- When startled or injured, she **puffs up** like a pufferfish.
- She has a recurring nightmare about SpongeBob's driving.
- Her first name is never officially confirmed in the series.

## The Krabby Patty

- The **Krabby Patty** is the Krusty Krab's signature burger and the most beloved food in Bikini Bottom.
- The **secret formula** is kept in a safe and is the subject of nearly every Plankton scheme.
- The secret formula has never been fully revealed on screen.
- SpongeBob is said to be the best fry cook the Krusty Krab has ever had.
- A Krabby Patty contains: **a bun, patty, lettuce, cheese, onions, tomatoes, ketchup, mustard, and pickles** (the arrangement is part of the secret).

## Episodes & Seasons

- The show has **13 seasons** as of 2024.
- The most critically acclaimed season is widely considered to be **Season 1 and 2** (1999–2001).
- The episode **"Band Geeks"** (Season 2) is frequently cited as the best episode — it ends with SpongeBob and friends performing at the Bubble Bowl.
- **"Chocolate With Nuts"** (Season 3) is another fan favourite, featuring SpongeBob and Patrick attempting to sell chocolate bars door to door.
- The infamous episode **"The Nasty Patty"** involves SpongeBob and Mr. Krabs believing they accidentally killed a health inspector.
- **"Rock-a-Bye Bivalve"** features SpongeBob and Patrick adopting a baby scallop.

## Movies

- The first theatrical film, **The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie**, was released in **2004**.
- In the movie, SpongeBob goes to **Shell City** to retrieve King Neptune's crown.
- The second film, **Sponge Out of Water**, was released in **2015** and features the characters coming to the surface world.
- The third film, **The SpongeBob Movie: Sponge on the Run**, was released in **2020** and features Patrick's snail Gary being taken to a snail spa called **Snail Park**.

## Fun Facts

- The voice of SpongeBob is provided by **Tom Kenny**, who has voiced the character since the very first episode.
- Bill Fagerbakke voices **Patrick Star**.
- Rodger Bumpass voices **Squidward**.
- Clancy Brown voices **Mr. Krabs**.
- Mr. Lawrence (also a writer on the show) voices **Plankton**.
- The show has inspired **memes**, internet culture moments, and even academic papers about its storytelling structure.
- **"Imagination"** — SpongeBob's rainbow hands gesture — became one of the most recognisable internet memes of the 2010s.
- The **"Mocking SpongeBob"** meme (Season 9, "Little Yellow Book") features SpongeBob walking like a chicken.
- Stephen Hillenburg passed away on **November 26, 2018**, from ALS (amyotrophic lateral sclerosis).
- Hillenburg had initially wanted the show to end after the 2004 movie, but Nickelodeon continued it without his involvement until he returned later.
